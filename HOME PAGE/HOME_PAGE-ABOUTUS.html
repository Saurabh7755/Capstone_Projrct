ABOUT JSPM
Jayawant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal (JSPM) was established in 1998 with the objective of creating centers of excellence for education in the field of Engineering, Medical, Pharmacy, Management, computers etc. This is by the vision of Prof. Dr. T.J.Sawant, an Electrical Engineer who thinks that, “Development of Techno–Entrepreneurial Society” is the key to achieve VISION 2020 as perceived by our Former PRESIDENT of India Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. Prof.Dr. T. J. Sawant is strongly convinced that establishment of excellent technical institutions is the solution to the problem of development of techno-economic society. Driven by a mission of providing an excellent quality education JSPM’s society has left no stone unturned in setting up Educational Institutes in Pune.At present there are five Campuses.
1.Tathawade

2.Hadapsar

3.Wagholi

4.Bavdhan

5.Narhe

6.Barshi
